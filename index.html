... Cacao Cake Recipe...

An indispensable snack for evening conversations. Wonderful cocoa cake with a few easy to make ingredients.Let 's do it!

Recipe:Mahir Türksoy

For How Many People :4-6 persons

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time :30 minutes

Ingredients For The Recipe of Cake With Cocoa

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1,5 cups of flour
  • 4 tablespoons of cacao
  • 1 package baking powder
  • 1,5 cups of milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 package vanilla extract

How to make cocoa cake?

  1. Prep your oven to 180 degrees
  2. Take your mixing bowl and first add the eggs, sugar, milk and oil. Mix it all together and beat it with a whisk for about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Next, apply cacao and flour little by little and mix it well.
  4. Apply oil to the cake mold and pour your mixture in it.
  5. Bake your mixture in the preheated 180 degrees oven for 30 minutes.
  6. After it is cooked, remove it from the oven and leave your cake for a chill off.
  7. After cooling, remove your cake from the mold and it’s ready! Bon Appétit!
  8. If you wish you can serve your cake with powdered sugar on it.
